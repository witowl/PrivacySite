Snorvia 隐私政策

生效日期：2026 年 5 月 23 日

概览

Snorvia 围绕本地优先的睡眠监测设计。默认情况下，你的睡眠记录、音频证据、导入铃声、设置和诊断日志都会存储在你的设备上。

我们不会自动上传音频。任何导出、分享、反馈邮件或 iCloud 同步行为，都需要你的主动选择或已开启的设置。

我们处理的信息

Snorvia 会在你的设备上保存睡眠记录、闹钟设置、睡眠计划、声音事件、环境噪音摘要、晨报、你导入的铃声和 App 偏好设置。

Snorvia 不要求创建账户，也不会出售你的个人信息。我们不会在 App 中使用第三方广告追踪器。

音频与声音分析

当你开始睡眠监测时，Snorvia 会使用麦克风在本机记录音频片段，并检测可能干扰睡眠的声音。音频文件和生成的声音片段会保留在你的设备上，除非你主动选择导出或分享。

Snorvia 可能使用 Apple 的设备端声音分析能力辅助标记声音。声音分类可能存在不确定性，因此 Snorvia 会采用保守分类，并且不会使用音频识别说话者身份。

HealthKit 数据

HealthKit 访问是可选的，并由你控制。如你授权，Snorvia 可能读取支持的睡眠、心率、呼吸频率和血氧数据，用于展示更丰富的睡眠摘要。

健康数据仅用于 App 内体验。Snorvia 不会将 HealthKit 数据用于广告写入，不会出售 HealthKit 数据，也不会将其用于医学诊断。

iCloud 同步

iCloud 同步是可选功能。开启后，Snorvia 会通过 Apple iCloud 同步睡眠记录和摘要数据，以便你的设备保持一致。

Snorvia 不会通过 iCloud 同步音频文件、铃声、收藏或设置。iCloud 数据由 Apple 按你的 Apple 账户和 iCloud 设置处理。

购买

购买和订阅由 Apple StoreKit 处理。Snorvia 会接收用于解锁功能的权益状态，但不会接收或保存你的完整支付卡信息。

诊断与反馈

Snorvia 会在本机保存诊断日志，用于排查 App 行为问题。诊断日志不会自动上传。

发送反馈时，你可以选择附带诊断日志。这些日志设计上不包含原始音频、HealthKit 原始样本、账户 token、绝对文件路径或敏感用户输入。

保留、导出与删除

数据会保留在你的设备上，直到你删除、自动保留设置清理符合条件的音频，或你卸载 App。收藏的声音片段会被保留，除非你主动删除。

你可以在“设置 > 权限与数据 > 数据管理”中删除音频、删除睡眠记录、清除本地数据，或导出隐私数据包。

权限

Snorvia 只会在你使用相关功能时请求所需权限，例如睡眠监测所需的麦克风、就寝提醒所需的通知、可选健康数据所需的 HealthKit，以及导入铃声所需的文件访问。

儿童

Snorvia 不面向儿童提供服务，我们不会有意收集儿童的个人信息。

非医疗建议

Snorvia 提供睡眠记录和个人洞察功能。它不是医疗设备，也不提供医疗建议、诊断或治疗。

政策变更

我们可能会随着 Snorvia 的变化更新本政策。如有重大变更，我们会更新生效日期，并在 App 内展示修订后的政策。

联系我们

如果你有隐私相关问题或请求，可以通过“设置 > 关于 > 邮件反馈”联系我们，或发送邮件至 app@lin.pm。如你选择附带诊断日志，请在发送前检查邮件内容。

Snorvia Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 23, 2026

Overview

Snorvia is designed around local-first sleep monitoring. Your sleep records, audio evidence, imported ringtones, settings, and diagnostic logs are stored on your device by default.

We do not upload audio automatically. Any export, sharing, feedback email, or iCloud sync action requires your choice or an enabled setting.

Information We Process

Snorvia stores sleep sessions, alarm settings, sleep plans, sound events, environment noise summaries, morning reports, ringtones you import, and app preferences on your device.

Snorvia does not require an account and does not sell your personal information. We do not use third-party advertising trackers in the app.

Audio and Sound Analysis

When you start sleep monitoring, Snorvia uses the microphone to record local audio segments and detect sounds that may disturb sleep. Audio files and generated clips remain on your device unless you choose to export or share them.

Snorvia may use Apple on-device sound analysis to help label sounds. Sound classification can be uncertain, so Snorvia uses conservative categories and does not use audio to identify a speaker.

HealthKit Data

HealthKit access is optional and controlled by you. If you grant permission, Snorvia may read supported sleep, heart rate, respiratory rate, and blood oxygen data to show richer sleep summaries.

Health data is used only inside the app experience. Snorvia does not write HealthKit data for advertising, does not sell HealthKit data, and does not use it for medical diagnosis.

iCloud Sync

iCloud sync is optional. If you turn it on, Snorvia syncs sleep records and summary data through Apple iCloud so your devices can stay consistent.

Snorvia does not sync audio files, ringtones, favorites, or settings through iCloud. iCloud data is handled by Apple under your Apple account and iCloud settings.

Purchases

Purchases and subscriptions are handled by Apple StoreKit. Snorvia receives entitlement status needed to unlock features, but we do not receive or store your full payment card details.

Diagnostics and Feedback

Snorvia keeps diagnostic logs locally to help troubleshoot app behavior. Diagnostic logs are not uploaded automatically.

If you send feedback, you may choose to attach diagnostic logs. These logs are designed not to include raw audio, HealthKit raw samples, account tokens, absolute file paths, or sensitive user input.

Retention, Export, and Deletion

Data remains on your device until you delete it, automatic retention settings remove eligible audio, or you uninstall the app. Favorite audio clips are preserved unless you delete them.

You can delete audio, delete sleep records, clear local data, or export a privacy data bundle from Settings > Permissions and Privacy > Data Management.

Permissions

Snorvia requests permissions only when needed for features you use, such as microphone access for sleep monitoring, notifications for bedtime reminders, HealthKit for optional health data, and file access for importing ringtones.

Children

Snorvia is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Not Medical Advice

Snorvia provides sleep tracking and personal insight features. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as Snorvia changes. When we make material changes, we will update the effective date and present the revised policy in the app.

Contact Us

If you have privacy questions or requests, contact us from Settings > About > Email Feedback or email app@lin.pm. If you include diagnostic logs, review the email before sending it.
